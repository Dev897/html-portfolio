The Best Movies According to Me

My Top Three Movies of all-time.

The Lion King

An epic story of how a son avenges his father's death commited by his Uncle. A family friendly movie.

Like Mike

An orphan who loves basketball, gets his hands on a pair of MJ sneakers from a donation. This helps him play basketball professionally and with some hurdles on the way.

The Dark Knight Rises

Most awaited Batman movie in my opinion, saw it first day, first show. Its the comeback of Batman against Bane his enemy in the movie.
